Poor Man's Cake Recipe

Description

As its name suggests the Poor Man's Cake was intended to be a sweet treat that could be enjoyed at a low cost. Thus, it was quite popular during the Depression era as many families were in a position where keeping costs low was a priority. This dish while not having many ingredients found in the traditional cake still provides amazing flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 cup raisins
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup shortening
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9X13 inch pan
  2. Mix raisins, water, brown sugar, and shortening in a saucepan. Bring to a oil and boil for 3 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, nutmeg, cloves, and salt in a large bowl. When raisin mixutre is cool, add to dry ingredients and mix well to combine.
  4. Bake in the preheated overn for 30 or 40 minutes. Don't overbake or it'll be too dry. Test after 30 mins. A toothpick inserted into the center of the cake should come out clean.
